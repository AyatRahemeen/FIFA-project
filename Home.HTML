What is FIFA?

The Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) is an international self-regulatory governing body of association football, beach soccer, and futsal. It was founded on 21 May 1904 to oversee international competition among the national associations of Belgium, Denmark, France, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, Sweden, and Switzerland. Headquartered in Zurich, Switzerland, its membership now comprises 211 national associations. These national associations must also be members of one of the six regional confederations: CAF (Africa), AFC (Asia), UEFA (Europe), CONCACAF (North and Central America and the Caribbean), OFC (Oceania), and CONMEBOL (South America).

The Biggest Players In Soccer

  • Cristiano Ronaldo

    • Why: Cristiano Ronaldo’s achievements place him among the greatest footballers ever: he has won five Ballon d’Or awards, five UEFA Champions League titles, 35 major trophies, and holds records for the most international goals (145) and most Champions League goals (140).

  • Lionel Messi

    • Why: Lionel Messi is the most decorated footballer in history, with a record 8 Ballon d’Ors, a 2022 World Cup, 2 Copa América titles, 4 Champions League trophies, and over 910 career goals, making him one of the greatest players ever.

  • Pelé

    • Why: Pelé is widely regarded as one of football’s greatest icons, the only player to win three FIFA World Cups, scorer of 1,279 career goals (a Guinness World Record), and a global ambassador who helped define “The Beautiful Game.”

  • Diego Maradona

    • Why: Diego Maradona is remembered as one of football’s greatest geniuses — the man who led Argentina to the 1986 World Cup, scored the “Hand of God” and the “Goal of the Century,” transformed Napoli into champions, and became a global cultural icon.

  • Ronaldinho

    • Why: Ronaldinho is one of football’s most gifted entertainers ever — a Ballon d’Or winner, two‑time FIFA World Player of the Year, World Cup champion, Champions League winner, Copa Libertadores winner, and the only player in history to win all major international and club competitions.
