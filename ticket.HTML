How hard is getting tickets?

It’s very hard to get FIFA World Cup tickets—demand massively exceeds supply, lotteries sell out quickly, and high‑profile matches (USA games, semifinals, final) are often gone before most fans ever get a chance. The 2026 tournament is the biggest in history, and millions of people are competing for a limited number of seats.

Why It’s So Difficult Extreme demand: Over 500 million ticket requests were submitted for about 7 million total tickets, meaning most applicants are rejected even after multiple attempts.

Lottery-based system: Early phases rely on random selection, so effort, timing, or loyalty doesn’t improve your odds.

High-demand matches: Finals, semifinals, and host‑nation games sell out instantly in first‑come phases.

Dynamic pricing: Prices rise with demand, making late purchases more expensive.
