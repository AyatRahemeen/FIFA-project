All The Awards that exist

Ballon d’Or: Awarded annually to the best male footballer in the world, established in 1956 by France Football. Lionel Messi holds the record with 8 wins, followed by Cristiano Ronaldo with 5 wins.

The Best FIFA Men’s Player: Presented by FIFA since 2017 to the top male player based on performance in the calendar year.

The Best FIFA Women’s Player: Recognizes the top female footballer annually, also managed by FIFA.

FIFA Puskás Award: Given for the best goal of the year, historically awarded to both male and female players, now male-only from 2024.

FIFA Marta Award: Honors the best goal by a female player annually.

Kopa Trophy: Awarded to the best young male player under 21.

Yashin Trophy: Recognizes the best male goalkeeper each year.

Ballon d’Or Striker of the Year: Awarded since 2021 to the top striker.

Ballon d’Or Coach of the Year: Introduced in 2024 for outstanding coaching achievements.

Team and Club Awards

FIFA Team of the Year: Annual recognition of the best national team players.

Ballon d’Or Club of the Year: Awarded to the club with the most Ballon d’Or nominees since 2021.

Club Champions from Europe: Honors top European club performances.

Other Notable Awards

FIFA Fair Play Award: Recognizes exemplary sportsmanship and fair play.

Socrates Award: Introduced in 2022 for contributions to football and society.

European Golden Shoe: Awarded to the leading goalscorer in European top divisions.

Women’s Football Awards

FIFA Women’s World Player of the Year: Recognizes the best female player annually.

FIFA Women’s World 11: Annual selection of the top eleven female players.

FIFA Fan Award: Celebrates outstanding moments as voted by fans.

These awards cover both individual excellence and team achievements, spanning global, continental, and club-level competitions.
